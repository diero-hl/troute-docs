# How to Get USDC.e

  To participate in the TROUTE presale, you need USDC.e on Tempo Chain. Here is how to get it.

  ---

  ## What is USDC.e?

  USDC.e is a bridged version of USDC native to Tempo Chain. It is the stablecoin that powers the entire Tempo Chain ecosystem and the only accepted payment token for the TROUTE presale. When you bridge USDC from Ethereum, Arbitrum, or Base to Tempo Chain, it arrives as USDC.e.

  USDC.e contract address on Tempo Chain: `0x20c000000000000000000000b9537d11c60e8b50`

  ---

  ## Step 1: Get USDC on a Supported Network

  Purchase USDC on any major exchange such as Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken, and withdraw it to your EVM wallet on Ethereum, Arbitrum, or Base. Make sure you are withdrawing USDC to the correct network that matches your wallet address.

  ---

  ## Step 2: Bridge to Tempo Chain

  Use one of the bridges below to move your USDC to Tempo Chain. It will arrive as USDC.e automatically.

  **Official Tempo Bridge (Recommended)**

  Go to bridge.tempo.xyz, connect your wallet, select USDC as the token, select Tempo Chain as the destination, enter the amount, and confirm. This is the easiest and most direct option.

  ```
  Bridge: https://bridge.tempo.xyz
  From:   Ethereum, Arbitrum, or Base
  To:     Tempo Chain (Chain ID: 4217)
  Token:  USDC to USDC.e
  Time:   2 to 10 minutes
  ```

  **relay.link (Alternative)**

  relay.link also supports Tempo Chain and often has competitive rates. Go to relay.link, select your source chain, select Tempo Chain as the destination, choose USDC, and complete the transfer.

  ```
  Bridge: https://relay.link
  From:   Ethereum or Arbitrum
  To:     Tempo Chain (Chain ID: 4217)
  Token:  USDC to USDC.e
  Time:   5 to 15 minutes
  ```

  ---

  ## Step 3: Add Tempo Chain to Your Wallet

  Before you can see your USDC.e balance or interact with the presale, your wallet needs to be connected to Tempo Chain. Add it manually in MetaMask or your preferred wallet using these settings:

  | | |
  |---|---|
  | Network Name | Tempo Chain |
  | RPC URL | https://rpc.tempo.xyz |
  | Chain ID | 4217 |
  | Currency Symbol | USD |
  | Block Explorer | https://explore.tempo.xyz |

  In MetaMask: click the network dropdown at the top, select Add Network, click Add a network manually, and enter the settings above.

  ---

  ## Step 4: Add USDC.e as a Custom Token

  To see your USDC.e balance in your wallet after bridging, add it as a custom token. In MetaMask go to Tokens, click Import tokens, and paste this contract address:

  ```
  0x20c000000000000000000000b9537d11c60e8b50
  ```

  Your wallet will auto-detect the symbol (USDC.e) and decimals (6).

  ---

  ## Step 5: Buy $TROUTE

  Once you have USDC.e on Tempo Chain and your wallet is connected to the Tempo network, go to the presale page at www.trouteprotocol.xyz, connect your wallet, enter the amount of USDC.e you want to spend, and confirm the transaction.

  Your $TROUTE tokens are sent directly to your wallet in the same transaction. There is no claiming process and no waiting period.

  ---

  ## Troubleshooting

  **I bridged but I cannot see my USDC.e.**
  Make sure your wallet is set to Tempo Chain (Chain ID 4217), not Ethereum or Arbitrum. Then add USDC.e as a custom token using the contract address above.

  **The bridge is taking a long time.**
  Bridges from Ethereum mainnet can take up to 15 minutes during periods of high congestion. Arbitrum is usually faster. If it has been more than 30 minutes, check your source chain transaction hash on the block explorer to confirm it was submitted.
