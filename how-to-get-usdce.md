# How to Get USDC.e

To participate in the TROUTE presale, you need USDC.e on Tempo Chain. Here is how to get it.

## What is USDC.e?

USDC.e is a bridged version of USDC on Tempo Chain. It is the only accepted payment token for the TROUTE presale.

USDC.e contract address on Tempo Chain: `0x20c000000000000000000000b9537d11c60e8b50`

## Step 1: Get USDC on a Supported Network

Purchase USDC on any major exchange (Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, etc.) and withdraw to your EVM wallet on Ethereum, Arbitrum, or Base.

## Step 2: Bridge to Tempo Chain

Use an official bridge that supports Tempo Chain to move your USDC across. Select USDC as the token and Tempo Chain as the destination network.

Bridge options will be listed here as they become available. Follow [@TROUTEprotocol](https://twitter.com/TROUTEprotocol) for updates on supported bridges.

## Step 3: Add Tempo Chain to Your Wallet

Add Tempo Chain manually to MetaMask or your preferred wallet:

| | |
|---|---|
| Network Name | Tempo Chain |
| RPC URL | https://rpc.tempo.xyz |
| Chain ID | 4217 |
| Currency Symbol | USD |
| Block Explorer | https://explore.tempo.xyz |

## Step 4: Buy $TROUTE

Once you have USDC.e on Tempo Chain, go to the presale page, connect your wallet, enter the amount you want to spend, and confirm the transaction.

Your $TROUTE tokens will be sent directly to your wallet immediately.
