  ## Token Utility

  **Payment Routing Fees**

  Every payment routed through the TROUTE protocol generates a 0.3% fee. This fee is distributed proportionally to all active $TROUTE stakers. As the number of AI agents using TROUTE grows, routing volume grows. As routing volume grows, fee revenue grows. The token is directly tied to protocol usage, not narrative.

  **Launchpad Trade Fees**

  Every buy and sell on the TROUTE token launchpad generates a 1% trade fee. That fee is split three ways: 80% goes to the token creator, 10% goes to $TROUTE stakers, and 10% goes to the TROUTE treasury. Every token launched on TROUTE contributes to staker yield from its first trade all the way to Enshrined DEX graduation and beyond.

  **Staking**

  $TROUTE holders stake their tokens to earn a share of all protocol fees: payment routing fees and launchpad trade fees. Staking is non-custodial. There are no artificial emission schedules or inflationary rewards. Yield comes entirely from real protocol revenue generated by real usage.

  **Governance**

  $TROUTE is the governance token of the protocol. Stakers vote on fee parameters, routing rules, supported assets, launchpad settings, and protocol upgrades. Governance begins minimal at launch and expands as the protocol matures. No decision that materially affects the protocol is made unilaterally.

  ## Why $TROUTE Has Value

  The token accrues value from two compounding sources.

  The first is payment routing. Every AI agent that integrates TROUTE routes payments and generates fees for stakers. As AI agent deployment scales globally, routing volume scales with it. More agents means more volume. More volume means more fees. More fees means more demand for $TROUTE.

  The second is the launchpad. Every token launched on TROUTE generates a $3 launch fee and continuous 1% trade fees that flow in part to stakers. As more humans and AI agents launch tokens through TROUTE, staker yield increases with every new project on the platform.

  Both revenue streams are live today on Tempo Chain mainnet. The protocol is not waiting for future development to generate value.
