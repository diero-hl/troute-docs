  ## Token Utility

  **Payment Routing Fees**

  Every payment routed through TROUTE generates a 0.3% fee paid to stakers. More AI agents using TROUTE means more routing volume. More routing volume means more fees. The token is tied to usage, not narrative.

  **Launchpad Trade Fees**

  Every buy and sell on the TROUTE launchpad generates a 1% fee. Split three ways: 80% to the token creator, 10% to $TROUTE stakers, 10% to the treasury. Every token launched on TROUTE adds to staker yield from first trade to graduation.

  **Staking**

  Stake $TROUTE to earn from all protocol fees: routing fees and launchpad trade fees. Non-custodial. No lock-up. No inflationary rewards. Yield comes from real protocol revenue.

  **Governance**

  $TROUTE stakers vote on fees, routing rules, and protocol upgrades. Governance starts minimal and expands as the protocol grows.

  ## Revenue

  Payment routing fees are live now. Launchpad trade fees are live now. Both go to stakers. The protocol does not need future development to generate revenue. It is generating it today.
