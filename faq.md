# FAQ

**When is the TGE?**

TGE date will be announced after the presale closes. Follow [@TROUTEprotocol](https://twitter.com/TROUTEprotocol) on Twitter for the official announcement.

**Is there a vesting period?**

No. Tokens are sent directly to your wallet at the time of purchase. There is no claiming process, no vesting schedule, and no lockup period.

**What wallet do I need?**

Any EVM-compatible wallet works. MetaMask is the most commonly used. Make sure your wallet is connected to Tempo Chain (Chain ID: 4217).

**What is the minimum purchase?**

The minimum purchase is $0.50 USDC.e.

**How do I get USDC.e on Tempo Chain?**

See the [How to Get USDC.e](how-to-get-usdce.md) page for a full step-by-step guide.

**Is the contract audited?**

See the [Security](security.md) page for full details on the contract and security measures.

**What happens if the hard cap is not reached?**

The presale runs until the hard cap of $30,000 USDC.e is reached or the presale period ends. Any funds raised will be used to seed the liquidity pool at TGE regardless of the final amount raised.

**Can I buy from any country?**

The presale is open to the public. It is your responsibility to ensure participation is legal in your jurisdiction. Do not participate if token presales are prohibited where you are located.

**Where do I see my $TROUTE tokens?**

Your tokens are sent directly to your wallet at purchase. To see them, add the TROUTE contract address to your wallet as a custom token: `0x72566b53a5ba0bef9de683e5074860e22705f8ea`

**Is there a team allocation?**

TROUTE is structured as a fair launch. The full 500,000,000 token supply is split between the presale (300M), liquidity pool (100M), and airdrop (100M) — all distributed directly to the community. No insider allocations.
